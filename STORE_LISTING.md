## Description (4000 chars max)

WordRealms combines the addictive word-finding gameplay of Wordscapes with the satisfaction of building your own fantasy kingdom.

CONNECT LETTERS, BUILD YOUR REALM
Swipe through 7 letters to form words. Every word you discover earns resources — wood, stone, and gold — that you use to construct buildings in your isometric fantasy world.

DAILY PUZZLES
A new puzzle every day, shared by all players worldwide. Complete the daily challenge to maintain your streak and climb the weekly leaderboard.

COMPETE IN LEAGUES
Rise through Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, and Diamond leagues. Earn ELO points by completing puzzles and ranking high on the daily leaderboard.

BATTLE PASS
Unlock exclusive building skins, avatar frames, and bonus resources with the seasonal Battle Pass.

6 LANGUAGES
Play in English, German, French, Spanish, Polish, or Turkish.
