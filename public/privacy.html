Privacy Policy — WordRealms

Last updated: April 2026

This policy describes how WordRealms (“we”, “the app”) handles information when you use our mobile and web application.

Data we collect

  • Anonymous gameplay statistics (e.g. progress, scores, streaks) stored locally and/or on our backend to sync your experience.
  • Account identifiers if you sign in (including anonymous sessions managed by our authentication provider).
  • Optional profile data such as a display name or email if you choose to provide them.
  • Technical data (e.g. device type, app version) as needed for stability and support.

Supabase

We use Supabase as a data processor for authentication and optional cloud storage. Data is processed according to Supabase’s terms and security practices. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Subscriptions (Battle Pass)

Purchases may be processed by RevenueCat and the applicable app store or payment provider. We do not receive your full payment card number.

Your rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you may have rights to access, rectify, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data, and to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. Contact us using the email below to exercise these rights.

Children

The app is suitable for ages 4+. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children beyond what is necessary for anonymous gameplay. If you believe we have collected data inappropriately, please contact us.
